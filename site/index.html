why this exists

Pretrained robot policies ship with paper numbers — but no apples-to-apples way to run them all.

Each release picks its own envs, seeds, success rule. lerobot-bench fixes the protocol — identical seeds, episode counts, CIs across the matrix — and publishes the disagreements as loudly as the agreements.

methodology snapshot

Three commitments that make the numbers comparable.

01 · SEEDING

Per-cell determinism contract.

Keyed by (policy, env, seed, ep_id); reruns reproduce the success vector bit-for-bit.

02 · INTERVALS

Wilson 95% CIs, Holm-Bonferroni.

Per-cell from N = 250; family-wise correction across 36 cells.

03 · MDE BAND

What 250 episodes can resolve.

Minimum detectable effect ~9pp at p ≈ 0.5.
